For this part of the program, the user has selected that they want to display an activity.  Once
the user has chosen this option, they will be prompted on what criteria they want to search for.  
This could mean searching for all activities by the activity name, the year, month or role.  For this
run, the user has chosen 'display', then the have chosen 'year' to display by.  The year chosen was 2013,
so all activities that have the year 2013 will be displayed to the user.
